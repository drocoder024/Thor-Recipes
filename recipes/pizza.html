Pizza Recipe

Description

The best pizza in the world is cooked by a proficient chef. The ingredients are handpicked and very tasty. It has olives, basil, mushroom, and it is delicious.

Ingredients :

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 3/4 cups bead flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
