Lasagna Recipe

Description

It is not about the type of meat which you are going to use to make your Lasagne. It is about how you are going to prepare it. This day recipe would require you a little bit of time but when you will eat this with family and friends after preparation then it would be worth the time.

Ingredients :

  • Egg Beaters (whole eggs) - 3/4 cup
  • Flour - 1 tablespoon
  • Parmesan Cheese - 3 tablespoons grated or ground
  • Ground Black Pepper - 1 teaspoon or so, as per your taste preference
  • Oil (vegetable oil) - 2 tablespoons
